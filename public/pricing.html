What's included — explained

📍 Monthly leads

Each business you export counts as one lead. Credits reset automatically on the 1st of each month. Unused credits do not carry over.

🤖 AI enrichment

Our AI visits each business website and extracts email addresses, owner names, and social media profiles. Each website visit uses one enrichment credit.

🔍 Essential filters

Filter results by: has/has no website, has/has no phone number, has/has no email, has/has no social profiles, open/closed status.

⚙️ Advanced filters

Available on Pro and Agency plans. Filter by minimum/maximum Google rating, number of reviews, and more to laser-target the right businesses.

📊 CSV export

Download your leads as a CSV file with one click — name, address, phone, website, email, owner, and all social profiles in one clean file ready for your CRM.

🔒 GDPR compliant

We only collect publicly available business data. All data is sourced from Google Maps and public business websites in accordance with UK and EU law.
